RFIs on-site Manual

MD ANDERSON - SUGAR LAND
Project 22041500 - ARC2416

Step 1. On ACC mobile App, go to the specific project:

1.1. Open a sheet
1.2. Use the toolbar on the right side of the drawing and select the “Cloud Tool.”
1.3. Place the cloud in the referenced area.
1.4. Select “Publish.”

Step 2. Create the RFI:

2.1. Select the cloud that was recently created again, and then select “Reference.”
2.2. In the window that appears, in the RFI section, select “+ Add.”
2.3. At the top, select “+ Create RFI.”
2.4. Fill in the important information for the RFI (title and question) and add photos if applicable.
2.5. In the upper-right corner of the box, select “Create.”
2.6. Then, return by selecting “Add references” in the upper-left corner and click “Add.”
2.6.1. Now the RFI Draft is created, let's move to your desktop

Step 3. On your Desktop

3.1. Go to the RFIs Module on the left side of ACC.
3.2. Open the Draft RFI that you just created.
3.3. Complete the remaining important information, such as the due date, cost/schedule impact, and any additional references.
3.4. Click on “Submit for review” and select the people who will review and respond to the RFI.
3.5. Finally, click on “Submit.”
3.5.1. Now the RFI is ready to be reviewed by the AOR/EOR

Step 4. Arcaise Procedure After RFI is Reviewed

4.1. Arcaise will process the RFIs following these steps:
4.1.1. Any additional locations not mentioned in the RFI will be identified and added.
4.1.2. The cloud markup will be replaced or supplemented with the official RFI markup in the identified locations.
